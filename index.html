I design products to help future generations.

I design products and conduct research that will help generations to come. That’s the longview of product design. If your digital or physical product has a longview then I’m available to help with research, interface, and experience design. My experience is cross-functional and I’ve developed products and services in education, ecology, social justice, sustainable energy, finance and more throughout my career. I’ve launched apps, published science, built exhibits, and communicated vision. If you need to improve adoption, engagement, functionality or reach with your product I have the skills and experience to help. I’ve mentored designers, led developers, and directed research all toward a shared vision of a better product.
