    Senior Product designer

    I started my design career developing media educational programs for a school in San Francisco. I've always been passionate about extended education and many of my projects have been transformative learning experiences for my users, my clients, and for myself. I worked for a Startup in San Francisco, Piper Computers, where I designed educational experiences for children learning electronics and programming. I founded a program for the Marine Megafauna foundation to encourage dive centers to participate in marine research. I designed material to educate divers and dive centers on the marine animals they were contributing to study. I've been a designer for almost 2 decades now, and I've got over half of that time focused on digital product. I'm commited to Coursera's mission, and have taken online courses at Coursera, Kahn Acadamy, Udemy, and through the California Community College system. I know I can make a signifigant impact
